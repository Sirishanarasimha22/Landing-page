Greeting card

A greeting card is a small piece of folded or flat cardstock designed to convey sentiments, well-wishes, or greetings for various occasions. These cards are typically exchanged between friends, family members, or acquaintances to mark special moments or to express feelings of love, appreciation, sympathy, or celebration..

Teddy bear

A teddy bear is a classic and beloved gift choice, often given to convey comfort, affection, and warmth. These soft, cuddly stuffed animals, typically made of plush material, feature a charming and huggable design. They serve as a symbol of love, companionship, and nostalgia, making them a cherished present for people of all ages..
